Who We Are

River of life Church of God is a spirit filled church in Lexington, Kentucky.
Our diverse and passionate community is dedicated to pursuing excellence and
excperiencing revival. We believe in a God who brings us FULL CIRCLE - from bad to good,
from depressed to victorious - He is the author of beautiful stories.

We offer various services, including a Spanish Service, and a free Men's Breakfast for
the community every Saturday. We truly LOVE JESUS, LOVE LEXINGTON, AND LOVE YOU!

Location: 757 Wilderness Road, Lexington, KY 40509
